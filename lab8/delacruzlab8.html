About our Trip

This summer, my wife and I went to New York City. We stayed in Brooklyn, did some sight seeing, and a had a great time! I wanted to share a few photos of the trip, and tell you a little bit about our favorite sites!


I spend quite a bit of time in NYC for work, so I am familiar with quite a few of the popular sites, and we wanted to try and explore more under the radar sites, and get a feel for a few individual neighborhoods, rather than see the big tourist-y things. We stayed in a neighborhood called Bushwick in Brooklyn, which is very young and trendy, and has a ton of bars, restaurants, and coffee shops. We felt like we picked the best place to stay on our trip, and could have spent our whole time in the neighborhood without running out of things to do!


One of our favorite things we did was visit Coney Island. We went on July 5th, there was a big fireworks display, and the whole boardwalk had an 80’s theme to celebrate the release of Stranger Things season 3. The atmosphere was so fun and this part of the trip is a memory I’ll hold on to for a long time!


Something that stands out to me about the city is the size and density of the buildings. I love seeing the grandness and oldness of all the buildings, as well as the mix of old and new. I hope you enjoy the pictures, and if you ever need any recommendations for things to do in NYC I would be happy to share more about my experiences!

Nathan De La Cruz: August 3rd
