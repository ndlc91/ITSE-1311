My Friend Bear

Who Bear Is

Bear is my 1.5 year old Golden Retriever. He is a big bundle of energy, never stops running around, and is the goofiest guy you'll ever meet.

What Bear means to Us

Bear is the first dog indoor dog that's been a part of my family. Growing up, we had dogs but they were never allowed in the house.

When me and my wife got married, we were excited about getting a dog once we got an apartment. We got bear when he was a puppy, and although puppies can be challenging, he has made us so happy!

Here are some of the benefits of owning a dog, as well as some of the challenges:

Pros

  • Lots of love
  • Always have a friend around
  • Makes friends everywhere he goes
  • Always down for cuddles

Challenges

  • Hair everywhere
  • Disobedient behavior
  • Hogs the bed
  • Sometimes pees in the house
