Mankind Forever first formed in a space between boredom and passion. Their first album “Songs For Someone” was as energetic and raw as their youth. A year later came their second album “Highs & Lows” — a mix of upbeat songs and dark, melancholic ballads, this album showcased their diverse writing ability.

After taking a hiatus to work on solo projects, Cole & Nathan are writing together again and are based out of Dallas, TX. Their goal is to write music that is timeless and bursts genres.

Their sound is changing, but still encompasses the passionate mood and lyrical sophistication of the first two albums. Only this time, it’s filled with indie-pop synth, fuller arrangements, and even more melodic riffs that command your attention.

Mankind Forever returned in July 2019 with eclectic single “One Day We’ll Look Down On The World We Created”. Their eagerly anticipated album is planned to release later this summer.
