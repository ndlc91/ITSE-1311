Vacation Details

We had a wonderfull winter vacation outside our own front door! It actually snowed this winter enough to do a bit of sledding and to build a snowman. Searching for vacation destinations on Google is a good idea.

Everyone was so excited about the snow that we spent all afternoon out and about playing in the snow. Even the dogs were excited and were playing around.

There are many locations that provide fun and inexpensive winter wonderland adventures.

  4. Feed the birds.
  5. Build a snowman.
